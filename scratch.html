Why I Exercise

To me, exercise helps me relieve stress and better myself. I like the idea of reaching my full potential in this life. One way to do that phsyically is by going to the gym regularly.

Workout Structure

  1. Push Day (Monday & Thursday)
    • Bench Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Triceps Pushdwons (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Shoulder Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Dips (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Pec Fly (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  2. Pull Day (Tuesday & Friday)
    • Lat Pulldowns (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Bicep Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Rows (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Hammer Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Rear Delt Flys (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  3. Leg Day
    • Warm Up: Adductor machine (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Squats or Hack Squat (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Calve Raises (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Hamstring Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
    • Quad Extensions (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
